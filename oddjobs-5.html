Job Finding App Concept

Information Systems 2 Assignment

We were tasked with making a concept app.

OddJobs App would be used to link those seeking employment and those who need a job to be done The aim behind this concept was to facilitate the process of finding jobs for anyone in need and to connect those people with the ones who need the jobs done, and hence we are creating OddJobs, a phone app that allows users to create ads for themselves for the jobs they can do and people who can request them to complete their jobs.

I enjoyed this project because we had to design a user interface. However the main purpose of the project was for us to understand Agile methods and managing sprints.
