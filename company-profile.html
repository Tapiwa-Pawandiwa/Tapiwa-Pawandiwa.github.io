Pawacons Company Profile

Engineering consultants comapany profile design

Pawacons Pty Ltd is a water engineering consulting company. I was tasked with designing their company profile from 2018 to date. I made sure to adhere to the company colors. This profile is used to market the company to potential clients in the engineering industry both government and private.

I made use of Canva templates, then I compiled images and edited the images to fit the overall company look.

I worked closely with the CEO who happens to be my father. I have maintained and updated the design when needed over the past 3 years. It has given me something interesting to do on the side while I study at Rhodes University.
