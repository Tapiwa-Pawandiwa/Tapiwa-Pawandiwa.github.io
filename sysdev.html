Apartment Rating System

Information Systems Group Project

Our task was to implement an apartment rating system for students in the form of a website.

As a group of 5 people . We were tasked with building and implementing an apartment rating system , where tenants can sign up , login and rate properties that they have stayed in. Tenants could rate , basic services such as water and electricity ,proximity to campus , safety and security, comfortability and work friendliness. The system also required an admin who acted as someone who would verify whether a tenant has actually stayed at the property that they are rating , including overall maintenance of the site. The platform also required Agents to upload and post property listings.

This was a very interesting project because we had to go through the entire software development cycle while making use of project management principles. We took a linear approach , these below were part of our deliverables in this year long assignment :

  • Domain study
  • Project Charter
  • Risk Register
  • Team Contracts

    • These below formed as part of our Systems Requirements Document:

  • Class Diagrams
  • Use Case Models
  • Activity Diagrams
  • State Machine Diagrams

    • These below formed as our Systems Design Specification Document:

  • User Interface Design
  • Database Design
  • Design Level Use Case Diagrams

This was a very interesting experience for we had to work as a team while pursuing our normal course work in various subjects.
