Green Cities Logo Design

Green Cities

In my first year , I took part in the Hults Prize Competition. Teams were tasked with coming up with an idea that would change the lives of 1 million people. So out of that came "Green Cities". I was part of a team of 4 people. Our idea revolved around solar panels and generating electricity for the less priveledged in a much more simpler manner that caters for different tax brackets.

The competition took place in 2017, we successfully made it to the regionals that were to be held in San Francisco but unfortunatetly we did not have the appropriate funding to attend.

Despite the dissapointment, that experience of coming up with an innovative idea and pitching it to very intelligent lecturers of the University sparked an entrepreneurial spirit within me that I did not know I possessed. For that I still hold this experience very dearly. I was also tasked with designing the logo.
